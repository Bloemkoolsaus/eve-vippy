Access Lists

You can create access-lists to grant access to your map(s) for:
  • Alliances or corporations not in your access-control-group
  • Any character with a VIPPY account (instead of just corporations/alliances)
Anyone granted access to your map via access-lists, and not in your access-control-group will not be able to see your online pilots and shiptypes!! They will only see pilots and shiptypes from their own access-control-group.
All other intel, such as known systems, notes, etc, is shown from your database!!
