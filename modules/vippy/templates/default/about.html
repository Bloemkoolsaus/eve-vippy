About VIPPY

VIPPY is made and maintained by Bloemkoolsaus.

Early 2012, I started building VIPPY because my corp (VFI) did not want to use Siggy anymore, but at the time there were no alternatives. I'm a programmer and my corp mates started to push me to build our own mapper, this would become VIPPY.

VIPPY started with only a couple of users (my own corp). It was never intended for to be used by anyone else other then us, but over the years, more and more people got exposed to VIPPY and now many groups of all sizes depend on it for their mapping needs.

VIPPY Features

A (very) brief overview of VIPPY's most important features.

A detailed map of the current chain(s), and quick intel for each system for maximum situational awareness!

  • System static(s)
  • Connection status
  • Wormhole effect
  • Who inhabites this system (if any)
